**FMCG Inventory Optimiser**

_Operational Decision Support Tool for Perishable Stock Management_

A data-driven inventory management application designed for UK FMCG (Fast-Moving Consumer Goods) professionals to balance the critical trade-off between stock availability and perishable waste mitigation.

Link: https://inventory-optimiser.streamlit.app

**The Problem**
In the UK food and drink sector, managing "Fresh" categories (like meat, dairy or produce) is a high-stakes balancing act.
- Over-ordering leads to massive financial losses through food waste (spoilage).
- Under-ordering leads to "out-of-stocks," empty supermarket shelves, and lost revenue.
- Most small-to-medium retailers still rely on manual spreadsheets that lack real-time "Waste Risk" alerts.

**The Solution**
This tool applies Engineering-level rigour to warehouse operations. It allows an Inventory Planner to:
- **Automate Reorder Points (ROP)**: Calculate exactly when to order based on daily demand and supplier lead times.
- **Visualise Waste Risk**: Instantly see if current stock levels exceed the product's shelf life.
- **Consolidate Buying Plans**: Build a multi-item "Market Basket" (e.g., Beef, Chicken, Milk) and export a total spend report for Finance approval.
- **Audit-Ready Logic**: Every metric includes a methodology tooltip to ensure transparency across Procurement and Operations teams.

**Key Features**
- **Smart Reorder Logic**: Integrated safety buffers to mitigate supplier lead-time uncertainty.
-**Shelf-Life Guardrail**: Real-time percentage calculation of "Days of Cover" vs. "Expiry Date."
- **Multi-Item Scenario Tracker**: Add multiple SKUs to a single session to create a unified procurement plan.
- **Financial Integration**: Automated "Total Planned Spend" calculations to assist with cash-flow forecasting.
- **Professional Export**: One-click CSV download for immediate use in Excel or ERP systems.

**Methodology & Logic**
- **ROP Calculation**: (Average Daily Sales × Lead Time) + 10% Safety Stock.
- **Waste Risk Analysis**: Threshold-based alerts triggered when stock cover exceeds 80% of total shelf life.
- **Landed Finance**: Cross-multiplication of ROP and Unit Cost to provide a "Capital Requirement" figure per order.

**Why this matters for your business**

Effective inventory management is the fastest way to increase a retailer's bottom line. By reducing waste by just 5%, a category manager can significantly improve the Net Profit Margin. This tool provides the "Operational Intelligence" to make those decisions with confidence.
